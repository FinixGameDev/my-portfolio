Finix's Portfolio

He does games and stuff

Hi! I'm Diogo Rocha aka Finix, and this is my portfolio. I'm a young game developer and game designer from Portugal and I recently graduated from IPCA studying Digital Games Development & Engineering.

If you're here, chances are you have heard my story a thousand times: I started my gaming journey pretty much in diapers, playing Max Payne on my dad's laptop, I then found my calling to game development at 14 when my middle school friends introduced me to the Unity Engine and so I've been learning about game dev and loving the medium ever since.

Anyways... here you can find some of the games and game development tools I've developed on my own, and some of the projects I had the pleasure of having a team to work with.

Highlighted Project

Sam's Last Nightmare

After a rescue operation gone bad Sam is left for dead in the middle of the street. Rescued by a man only known as The Doctor and without memories Sam now spends his nights trying to remember what happened in that damned operation. Each time remembering a different way.

Find your way through Sam's memories and explore a desolate city in order to reach the truth in this rogue-like survival-horror. Explore three distinct locations:

  • A big open city district;
  • A procedural generated sewer system;
  • An hospital that shifts it's layout in each run;

With only a machete as your only friend scavenge the lost city in search of supplies for both you and your flashlight. Keeping the lights on means keeping those who lurk in the shadows away.

Experience an unique story with 5 different endings, each one bringing you closer to the truth of Sam's last nightmare and his forsaken world.
